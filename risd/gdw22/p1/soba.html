SOBA WITH SCALLION NANBAN SAUCE
THE RECIPE INDEX
NOTES
The thick soy sauce in Kaeshi and ingredients, such as chicken and leeks give umami to the dipping sauce, intended for winter.

Serves 4.

INGREDIENTS
soba

for 4 persons
g chicken meat
large scallions
cc dark soy sauce
cc thick soy sauce
g sugar
cc mirin
cc thick katsuo dashi
cc sake
sansho
mustard
egg yolk

METHOD
1.
Prepare “kaeshi,” a part of the dipping sauce with the dark soy sauce, thick soy sauce, sugar and mirin. Boil them up. (Kaeshi)
2.
Cut thick scallions into 5 cm lengths.
3.
Dilute 1 with thick katsuo dashi and sake.
4.
Add bite-sized chicken (2~3cm cube) and 2. Cook for 12 minutes.
5.
Cook the soba and drain. Serve with 4. Add sansho, mustard and egg yolk as you like.

SOURCE
Chef Takeshi Kawanishi, restaurant Hisago Zushi, Arima, Kobe, Japan
